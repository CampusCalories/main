Campus Calories

We want eating healthy on campus to be effortless.

Overview

Campus Calories is an Android Application designed for students attending The University of Alabama who desire simple and easily accessible nutritional information from both on and off campus dining locations.

Structure

Upon opening, the application launches a Google Map-based activity that marks the available dining locations on campus. These locations can be selected to retrieve more information about the location itself, or any relavent nutritional information about meals there. Outside of the mobile application. CampuCalories offers the option for non-Campus Dining Locations to add their store information to our application. This can be done through our website interface.
